About

A research profile built around safer generative AI.

I am a Ph.D. student at Nanjing University of Aeronautics and Astronautics, advised by Prof. Yushu Zhang. I am currently studying as a visiting Ph.D. scholar in Milano, Italy, under the supervision of Prof. Elena Ferrari.

My work explores generative concept security, uncertainty analysis, causal analysis, personalized text-to-image generation, content toxicity detection, diffusion models, and foundation models.
